## Purpose

This package supports the formal close of Rudi's AED 1,000,000 investment into ZAAHI. All documents are designed for the Monday 2026-04-21 meeting and the 6–8 week formal close that follows.

ZAAHI is an operating company with a live production platform (`zaahi.io`) and a concrete commercial plan. The documents in this package reflect that maturity. ### Positioning note

The package is built around a single goal: **Rudi reads these documents and has no reason not to sign.** Every clause has been calibrated to maximum minority-investor comfort within ranges that remain reasonable for the founders. Key comfort features include: one board seat of three, 1.5× non-participating liquidation preference, Most Favoured Nation terms, redemption right after seven years, super pro-rata rights up to 15 %, full anti-dilution protection to IPO, exclusion from any drag-along, and comprehensive information rights (monthly summaries, quarterly reports, annual audited statements, optional real-time bank read-access, and immediate major-event notifications).

## Q&A preparation — likely Rudi questions

### On ZAAHI product

**Q: What is actually live today?**
A: `zaahi.io` is in production with 114 parcels loaded, 556,000 plots rendered across Dubai + Abu Dhabi + Oman via PMTiles, full 3D ZAAHI Signature visualisation, Phase 1 user dashboards (Owner, Buyer, Broker), Ambassador program with tiered memberships, Archibald AI assistant, feasibility calculator, and the complete deal-engine data model. The successful demo on 2026-04-17 validated all core flows.

**Q: How much of the Master Tree is built?**
A: Approximately 6–8% by section count, higher by user-visible functionality weight. 2 of 85 sections are fully built (§18 Referrals, §77 Web Platform for MVP scope); 19 are partially built; 16 have scaffolding; 48 are not yet started. This is documented rigorously in the gap analysis (`docs/architecture/research/ZAAHI_CURRENT_STATE_VS_MASTER_TREE.md` on `docs/master-tree-v3` branch).

**Q: What makes ZAAHI different from Bayut, Property Finder, or Huspy?**
A: Bayut and Property Finder are listings portals. Huspy is a mortgage + brokerage vertical. ZAAHI is a horizontal operating system — the full 85-section Master Tree vision spans assets, participants, transactions, technology, infrastructure, finance, governance, intelligence, ecosystem, and operations. We are not competing in listings. We are building the platform that listings portals, brokerages, and institutions will eventually integrate with.

### On the investment

**Q: Why AED 10M post-money valuation?**
A: It yields Rudi 10% ownership for a AED 1,000,000 commitment in the cleanest arithmetic. The valuation is defensible against: (a) comparable early-stage UAE proptech rounds, (b) the concrete platform already in production (≈18 months of senior-engineer development), (c) RERA-licensed founders with combined 35 years of real estate experience. It is deliberately conservative — a later priced round (Series Seed 6–12 months out) will crystallise a higher number as platform traction scales.

**Q: What does "full anti-dilution to IPO" mean for Rudi?**
A: Rudi's 10% stake in both Agency (Dubai Mainland LLC) and Platform (ADGM HoldCo) is protected against dilution from all future equity issuances, up to and including IPO. New rounds dilute Zhan's share (with a 10% floor) and the company treasury pool. This is documented in detail in `TERM_SHEET.md` §7 and `MOU_RUDI.md` §3.

**Q: What happens if the platform company is never formed?**
A: The Platform (ADGM HoldCo) opens only after the first agency deal closes. If no agency deal closes within 12 months, the AED 1M commitment still secured Rudi's 10% of the Agency (Dubai Mainland LLC), and the Platform arrangement is simply deferred. No funds are lost; no obligations are unmet.

**Q: What's the exit path?**
A: Three scenarios modelled in `FINANCIAL_MODEL_V1.md` Tab 5 "Distribution Waterfall":
- M&A by a strategic (Property Finder / Bayut / Huspy / Careem-class): most likely in years 3–5.
- Series A–C growth equity rounds: secondary liquidity opportunity for Rudi at each.
- IPO (years 5–10): Master Tree full platform scale.

### On governance

**Q: Do I get a board seat?**
A: Yes. The Board of Directors of both entities consists of three members: Zhan, Dymo, and Rudi. Decisions are by simple majority (2 of 3). Rudi has no veto right — the founders together can always outvote Rudi, and Rudi can ally with either founder to carry any motion in a founder disagreement. This is the maximum-comfort governance position for a 10 % silent-partner investor.

**Q: Can I block a decision?**
A: No motion requires Rudi's consent. The Shareholders Agreement is explicitly designed without investor veto rights, to preserve founder operational authority. However, two protections operate outside the board:
- The Shareholders Agreement itself cannot be amended without Rudi's written consent (because he is a party to it).
- If Rudi's economic rights are impaired in a way that contradicts the MFN or anti-dilution clauses, the impaired clauses are unenforceable against him and automatically reverted.

**Q: When do I receive dividends?**
A: Quarterly, per the Dividend Policy codified in the SHA (Option B — see `PROFIT_DISTRIBUTION_MECHANICS.md`). Rudi receives 10% of declared net profits from each agency deal closed, after the reserve threshold is satisfied. First expected distribution Q3 2026 (Jul–Sep), following the first closed deals.

**Q: When do I receive financial reports?**
A: A four-layer information rights package:
- **Monthly** — concise management summary (within 15 days of month end): revenue, deals in pipeline, deals closed, cash position, key KPIs.
- **Quarterly** — full unaudited management accounts (within 45 days of quarter end): P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, commentary.
- **Annually** — audited financial statements (within 90 days of year end).
- **Real-time (optional)** — read-only access to operating bank account statements if Rudi elects.
- **Immediate** — notification within 48 hours of any material event (litigation, regulator action, financing over AED 500,000, key-person departure, acquisition offer received).

**Q: Can I be dragged into a sale I don't want?**
A: No. Rudi is expressly excluded from any drag-along obligation. Even if Zhan and Dymo accept a buyer for the entire company, Rudi may hold his shares or sell on the same terms at his sole discretion. This is unusual minority protection and is offered explicitly for Rudi's comfort.

**Q: What if future investors get better terms than me?**
A: Most Favoured Nation clause applies. If any future investor receives any economic term more favourable than Rudi's (e.g., higher liquidation preference, deeper anti-dilution, better redemption terms), Rudi automatically receives the same term at no additional cost. Applies to every SAFE, preferred share, or convertible issued for 24 months after Rudi's investment.

**Q: Can I exit my investment?**
A: Yes, via three independent routes:
1. **Natural exit** — M&A, Series rounds, or IPO (most likely scenario). Rudi participates pro-rata.
2. **Market transfer** — after Year 2, Rudi may sell his shares subject to a standard Right of First Refusal to the Company and other shareholders.
3. **Redemption** — from Year 7, Rudi may request buyback at Fair Market Value determined by a mutually-appointed valuer. The Company has 12 months from the redemption notice to arrange financing. This is a guaranteed exit floor.

**Q: Can I increase my stake in future rounds?**
A: Yes. Rudi has **super pro-rata rights** up to 15 % — he may invest additional capital in future rounds beyond his pro-rata entitlement, to grow his stake from 10 % to up to 15 %. This is a right, not an obligation.

### On the team

**Q: What if Zhan or Dymo leaves?**
A: Both founders vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff. Rudi's 10% remains fully vested. If a founder departs voluntarily, their unvested shares return to the company treasury. Pre-agreed successor protocol applies for the CEO/CTO role. Key-person insurance is planned for Q3 2026 once entities are formed.

**Q: Why are Zhan and Dymo the only shareholders beside me?**
A: They are the only parties with unconditional commitment to ZAAHI today. Future hires (first broker, first engineer) will receive equity from a dedicated Employee Option Pool carved from the company treasury — at terms that preserve Rudi's 10% anti-dilution.

## Non-negotiable principles

These are the principles informing every document in this package. They are not up for re-negotiation Monday; only the exact mechanics within them are.

1. **Rudi is a silent-partner investor with board representation.** One board seat of three (no veto; majority rules). No operational role. No consent required for ordinary-course business. Comprehensive transparency (monthly, quarterly, annual, real-time optional), 10 % of profits, protected equity with 1.5× liquidation preference, MFN, redemption right from Year 7, super pro-rata up to 15 %, excluded from drag-along.
2. **Zhan holds absolute operational authority over the platform** (ADGM HoldCo), protected by a 10% floor and performance bonus shares.
3. **Dymo holds absolute operational authority over the agency** (Dubai Mainland LLC), protected by 45% equity in OpCo.
4. **Every agency deal profit is split 10/10/10/70.** Rudi, Dymo, Zhan — 10% each. The remaining 70% funds platform development.
5. **ZAAHI is one company, two entities.** The agency is the immediate revenue engine; the platform is the long-horizon asset. Neither is separable from the other in the founder vision.
6. **Self-sovereignty is architectural.** Every piece of tooling ZAAHI uses is chosen with a migration path to own infrastructure.
7. **Additive only for Master Tree.** 85 sections are the canonical surface; we add, we refine, we never delete.

## Gaps requiring professional legal review

These flagged items cannot be resolved without UAE-qualified counsel and are deliberately left open in the drafts:

- **SAFE tax treatment under UAE CT regime.** The Post-Money SAFE is a US-origin instrument; its characterisation under UAE Federal Decree-Law 47/2022 (Corporate Tax) and VAT law needs counsel sign-off.
- **Performance bonus share triggers — exact contractual language.** Drafted in plain English; formal SHA clause language must be attorney-authored.
- **Dividend Policy — exact SHA clause language.** Drafted mechanics; clause language and board-resolution flow must be attorney-authored.
- **Anti-dilution formula specifics.** "Full ratchet" is the clear intent; weighted-average vs broad-based-weighted-average alternatives need counsel confirmation for enforceability in ADGM and Mainland.
- **Inter-company funding mechanism.** How does 70% agency net profit flow to the platform company? Service agreement, capital contribution, or shareholder loan — each has different tax and cross-border implications.
- **RERA broker licence status during transition.** Individual broker cards (held by Zhan and Dymo today) are not transferable to company cards. The exact sequence of surrendering individual cards and issuing company cards must be choreographed with DLD.
- **Abu Dhabi nexus.** Al Jurf office triggers ADREC (Abu Dhabi Real Estate Centre) and Abu Dhabi Department of Municipalities and Transport rules. If office is physically in Al Jurf, a Dubai Mainland LLC requires an AD branch — or the entity is established in AD directly. Counsel to advise optimal structure.
