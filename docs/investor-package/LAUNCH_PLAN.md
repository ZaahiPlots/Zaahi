This Launch Plan operationalises the commitments in the Memorandum of Understanding, Term Sheet, and Executive Summary. ## Risk flags and mitigations

- **Risk:** RERA broker licence application delayed beyond Week 8.
  **Mitigation:** Start application Week 3 (8 weeks ahead of target); maintain relationship with DLD/RERA contacts; use boutique law firm for fast-track.

- **Risk:** Bank account opening takes longer than 8 weeks.
  **Mitigation:** Apply in parallel at two banks (Mashreq Neo + Emirates NBD); temporarily hold Rudi's AED 1 M in founder-controlled escrow with signed legal trust agreement during any gap.

- **Risk:** First agency deal takes longer than 4 months.
  **Mitigation:** Pipeline built in Phase 2 with 20+ prospects; founder network activation; Archibald AI lead qualification; pricing competitive per market norms.

- **Risk:** Dubai Mainland LLC cannot lease in Al Jurf (Abu Dhabi jurisdiction).
  **Mitigation:** Legal advice Week 1 confirms structure; fallback to Abu Dhabi Mainland entity or Abu Dhabi branch of Dubai LLC; all options budgeted in Phase 1.

- **Risk:** Founder burnout during Year 1.
  **Mitigation:** Salary floors per `ZHAN_PROTECTIONS.md`; first agent hire Month 3; second agent Month 7; clear split of responsibilities (Dymo agency, Zhan platform).

- **Risk:** Platform development starves while agency takes priority.
  **Mitigation:** 70 % of agency net profit flows to Platform Development Fund per SHA Dividend Policy; Zhan retains protected time for platform work; quarterly platform-progress reviews.
