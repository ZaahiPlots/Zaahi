## Preamble

This Memorandum of Understanding reflects a considered partnership between the founders of ZAAHI — a fully operating technology company with a live production platform at `zaahi.io` — and Rudi, a principal investor whose contribution enables ZAAHI's structured expansion into agency operations in Al Jurf while protecting both platform development and investor economic interests.

The Parties have agreed in principle that Rudi will invest AED 1,000,000 (one million United Arab Emirates dirhams) to formalise a partnership across two legal entities: the agency (to be incorporated as a Dubai Mainland LLC with registration commencing Monday 2026-04-21) and the platform (to be incorporated as an ADGM entity upon closing of the first agency deal, targeted September 2026).

This MOU records the principal terms on which the Parties intend to proceed. Except for Clauses 10 (Exclusivity) and 11 (Confidentiality), **this MOU is NOT a binding agreement** with respect to the investment, equity structure, or any other commercial term. Exact contractual language is subject to UAE counsel review and will be embodied in:

- a Post-Money SAFE (Y Combinator 2018 standard, adapted for UAE / ADGM law), to be executed within one week of this MOU (target: Week 1, Apr 28 – May 4); and
- a formal Shareholders Agreement, to be executed within 6–10 weeks of this MOU following entity formation and counsel drafting.

## 1. The Investment

Rudi intends to invest AED 1,000,000 (the "**Investment**"), to be paid in a single tranche upon execution of the Post-Money SAFE. The Investment shall be allocated 100 % to agency launch (entity formation, RERA licensing, Al Jurf office, first agent hire, marketing launch, working capital). Platform company operations will be funded from agency net profits thereafter.

## 3. Anti-Dilution Protection

- **Rudi and Dymo** shall be entitled to **full anti-dilution protection** on both Agency and Platform entities up to and including an initial public offering. Any future equity issuance that would dilute their ownership percentage below the levels in Clause 2 shall either: (a) trigger proportional bonus issuance to Rudi and Dymo at no additional cost to them, or (b) be prohibited without their written consent.
- **Zhan** shall dilute with future Series rounds in the ordinary course, subject to a **10 % floor**: Zhan's equity in either entity shall not fall below 10 % by ordinary-course issuance. This floor is independent of Zhan's Performance Bonus Shares (see `ZHAN_PROTECTIONS.md` §1).

## 4. Profit Distribution (Agency, per deal)

| **Dymo** | 10 % |
| **Zhan** | 10 % |

| **Total** | 100 % |

Distribution occurs quarterly pursuant to the Dividend Policy codified in the Shareholders Agreement (Option B — see `PROFIT_DISTRIBUTION_MECHANICS.md`). The first distribution is expected in Q3 2026 following the first closed deals.

## 5. Rudi's Protection Package

In consideration of the silent-partner nature of Rudi's participation, the Parties agree the following institutional-grade minority-investor protections will be codified in the Post-Money SAFE and the Shareholders Agreement:

- **Governance.** Rudi shall hold **one board seat of three** on each of Agency and Platform. The Board shall consist of Zhan, Dymo, and Rudi. Decisions shall be by **simple majority (2 of 3)**. Rudi **shall have no veto right** over any ordinary-course matter. This reflects the intent that the founders operate ZAAHI and Rudi participates as a silent-partner investor with observational rights.
- **Liquidation preference.** **1.5× non-participating** liquidation preference on conversion of the SAFE to preferred shares. In a liquidity event, Rudi shall receive the greater of (a) 1.5× the Investment amount, or (b) his pro-rata share of distributable proceeds.
- **Most Favoured Nation.** For 24 months following the Investment, if the Company issues any equity, convertible security, or SAFE on terms more favourable to that investor than those granted to Rudi, Rudi shall automatically be entitled to the more favourable terms at no additional cost.
- **Redemption right (Year 7).** From the seventh anniversary of the Investment, Rudi may deliver a redemption notice to the Company. Upon receipt, the Company shall have 12 months to repurchase Rudi's shares at Fair Market Value, determined by a mutually-appointed valuer (or in absence of agreement, by the Dubai International Arbitration Centre). This operates as a guaranteed exit floor independent of M&A timing.
- **Super pro-rata rights.** On any future equity financing, Rudi may elect to invest beyond his pro-rata entitlement up to a maximum cumulative holding of 15 % in each entity.
- **Excluded from drag-along.** Rudi shall be expressly excluded from any drag-along obligation. Even where the founders accept a buyer for the entire Company, Rudi may hold his shares or sell on the same terms at his sole discretion.
- **Standard tag-along.** On any founder transfer of > 25 % of their collective holdings, Rudi may tag along on the same terms on a pro-rata basis.
- **Right of First Refusal.** On any proposed transfer of Rudi's shares after Year 2, the Company and the other shareholders (pro-rata) shall have a 30-day right of first refusal on matching terms.

## 6. Information Rights

The Company shall provide Rudi with:

- **Monthly** — concise management summary (within 15 days of month end): revenue, deals in pipeline, deals closed, cash position, key KPIs.
- **Quarterly** — full unaudited management accounts (within 45 days of quarter end): P&L, balance sheet, cash flow, commentary.
- **Annually** — audited financial statements (within 90 days of year end).
- **Real-time** — read-only access to operating bank account statements (at Rudi's election; the Company will arrange with the operating bank to enable this access).
- **Immediate** — notification within 48 hours of any material event, defined as: litigation or dispute > AED 100,000; regulator investigation or sanction; any financing transaction > AED 500,000; acquisition or partnership offer received; key-person departure (Zhan or Dymo); material breach of any material contract.

## 7. Founder Vesting and Protections

- Zhan and Dymo shall subject their existing and future shares to **4-year reverse vesting with a 1-year cliff**, starting from the Effective Date of the Shareholders Agreement. Single-trigger acceleration on involuntary termination without cause; double-trigger acceleration (change-of-control + termination) on acquisition.
- Rudi's shares shall be **fully vested immediately** upon execution of the Post-Money SAFE.
- The Parties separately acknowledge and intend to codify in the Shareholders Agreement the Zhan-specific protection package set out in `ZHAN_PROTECTIONS.md`, namely: (i) Performance Bonus Shares (+5 % per trigger, maximum 15 % cumulative), (ii) Founder Salary Floor (AED 30,000–50,000 per month), and (iii) Tag-Along + Right of First Refusal on any Rudi share transfer.

## 8. Pre-Existing Intellectual Property

Zhan shall irrevocably assign to the Platform Company (ADGM HoldCo), on or before the Effective Date of the Shareholders Agreement and at no additional consideration, all pre-existing intellectual property related to ZAAHI, including without limitation: the software codebase of `zaahi.io`; the Master Tree v3 document and derivative architecture documents; the ZAAHI brand, wordmark, and logo; the 3D ZAAHI Signature visualisation methodology; the 114 parcels database; the PMTiles coverage (Dubai + Abu Dhabi + Oman); the Ambassador program architecture; the Phase 1 user dashboards; the Archibald AI integration; the domain `zaahi.io` and related domains and social handles; and all related IP that Zhan personally owns or controls as of the date of this MOU.

Zhan represents and warrants that he owns all such IP free of any encumbrance and that the assignment may be completed without third-party consent.

## 10. Exclusivity (BINDING)

For a period of thirty (30) days from the date of this MOU, the Parties shall not solicit, negotiate, or enter into any agreement with any third party on terms substantially equivalent to those set out in this MOU. This Clause is binding on all Parties.

## 11. Confidentiality (BINDING)

Each Party shall hold in confidence all information received from another Party relating to the proposed Investment, the ZAAHI business, the financial model, the Master Tree architecture, customer or supplier lists, and any other non-public information, and shall not disclose such information to any third party except: (a) to professional advisers bound by professional confidentiality obligations; (b) to entities owned or controlled by the disclosing Party; or (c) as required by law, regulation, or court order (with prompt notice to the other Parties where legally permissible).

This Clause is binding on all Parties and shall survive the termination of this MOU for a period of three (3) years.

## 12. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This MOU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM), which apply English common law principles. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this MOU shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration under the rules of the ADGM Arbitration Centre (or, at the disputing Parties' mutual election, the Dubai International Arbitration Centre). The seat of arbitration shall be Abu Dhabi. The language of arbitration shall be English.

## 13. Entire Understanding; Supersession

This MOU records the Parties' entire understanding with respect to the subject matter as of the date of execution. It supersedes all prior discussions, correspondence, and representations, whether oral or written. It is superseded by the Post-Money SAFE upon execution of that instrument, and further by the Shareholders Agreement upon its execution.

## 14. Non-Binding Nature of Commercial Terms

The Parties acknowledge that, except for Clauses 10 (Exclusivity) and 11 (Confidentiality) which are binding, all other provisions of this MOU are non-binding statements of intent. No Party shall have any liability to any other Party for failing to reach agreement on the formal Post-Money SAFE or Shareholders Agreement, provided each Party has negotiated in good faith.

## 15. Counterparts and Electronic Signature

This MOU may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall constitute an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
