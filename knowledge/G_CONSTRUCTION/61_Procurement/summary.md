# 61 Procurement


61 Procurement refers to the structured process of acquiring goods, services, or works for real estate projects, critical for ensuring cost efficiency, compliance, and quality in Dubai’s real estate sector. Key concepts include vendor selection, contract management, cost control, risk mitigation, and compliance with UAE regulations. The Zaahi platform leverages technology to streamline procurement, enhancing transparency and efficiency. UAE regulations mandate adherence to Law No. 8 of 2010 (Procurement Law) and the Public Procurement Regulatory Authority (PPRA) guidelines, emphasizing transparency, competitive bidding, and accountability. Technology applications include blockchain for contract tracking, AI-driven vendor risk assessments, IoT for asset management, and digital tender platforms. Business model relevance lies in reducing operational costs, improving vendor relationships, and ensuring regulatory compliance. Required data points for the platform include vendor credentials, project specifications, budget forecasts, compliance certifications, and real-time procurement analytics.
