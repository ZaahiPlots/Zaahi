# 21 Contractors


21 Contractors is a digital platform within the Zaahi ecosystem in Dubai UAE, designed to connect real estate developers with construction contractors, streamline project execution, and ensure compliance with local regulations. Key concepts: 1. Digital marketplace for construction services, 2. Project management tools for real-time tracking, 3. Supplier network integration, 4. Real-time bidding and contract management, 5. Compliance checks for UAE construction standards, 6. Integration with Zaahi’s real estate data, 7. Sustainability and energy efficiency metrics, 8. Smart contracts for automated payments, 9. IoT-enabled site monitoring, 10. AI-driven risk assessment. UAE regulations: Adherence to the Dubai Economic Vision 2021, Construction Law (Federal Law No. 11 of 2018), Real Estate Regulatory Agency (RERA) guidelines, and data privacy laws (Federal Law No. 4 of 2020). Technology applications include blockchain for contract transparency, cloud-based collaboration, and AI for predictive project analytics. Business model relevance: Positions 21 Contractors as a B2B intermediary, reducing developer costs and improving contractor efficiency. Required data points: Project specifications, contractor credentials, compliance certifications, real-time bidding records, performance metrics, user feedback, and contract lifecycle management.
