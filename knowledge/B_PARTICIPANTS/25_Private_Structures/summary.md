# 25 Private Structures


**Definition**: 25 Private Structures refer to a curated set of 25 exclusive, privately owned real estate units within Dubai’s real estate market, typically characterized by high-end, limited availability, and bespoke ownership models. These structures are often part of luxury developments, private villas, or specialized property categories, offering unique investment or lifestyle opportunities.  

**Key Concepts**:  
- **Ownership Models**: Freehold, leasehold, or shared equity ownership for private structures.  
- **Legal Frameworks**: Compliance with UAE Real Estate Regulatory Authority (DRERA) laws, including property registration, transfer, and taxation.  
- **Investment Attributes**: High liquidity, capital appreciation potential, and exclusivity.  
- **Smart Property Integration**: IoT-enabled systems for energy management, security, and automation.  
- **Privacy and Security**: Enhanced confidentiality measures, such as anonymous ownership or restricted access.  
- **Customization**: Tailored interior designs, amenities, and location-specific features.  
- **Market Segmentation**: Targeted toward high-net-worth individuals (HNWIs), investors, or niche demographics.  
- **Sustainability**: Green building certifications (e.g., LEED, Estidama) for eco-friendly structures.  
- **Technology-Driven Valuation**: AI-driven pricing models and predictive analytics for market trends.  
- **Digital Twin Integration**: Virtual replicas for property visualization and management.  

**UAE Regulations**:  
- **DRERA Compliance**: All private structures must be registered with DRERA, ensuring adherence to licensing, transparency, and dispute resolution protocols.  
- **Ownership Limits**: Freehold ownership is restricted to UAE nationals or entities; foreign ownership requires a local partner.  
- **Taxation**: Property tax (5% of market value) applies to freehold properties, while leasehold units are taxed differently.  
- **Zoning Laws**: Structures must align with Dubai’s zoning regulations, including permissible land use and building codes.  
- **Data Privacy**: GDPR-compliant data handling for user and property information.  
- **Smart City Standards**: Adherence to Dubai’s Smart City framework for technology integration.
