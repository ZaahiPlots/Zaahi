# 85 Accessibility


85 Accessibility refers to the implementation of design and technological standards ensuring properties are usable by individuals with physical, sensory, or cognitive disabilities. In the context of Zaahi, a Dubai UAE real estate platform, it involves aligning property listings, infrastructure, and digital tools with accessibility requirements to enhance inclusivity.  

Key concepts include:  
1. **Barrier-Free Design**: Compliance with UAE standards for ramps, elevators, tactile paving, and accessible parking.  
2. **Universal Design Principles**: Creating environments adaptable to diverse needs without specialized retrofitting.  
3. **Assistive Technology Integration**: Smart home systems, voice-activated controls, and emergency alert mechanisms.  
4. **Digital Accessibility**: Ensuring platform interfaces are compatible with screen readers, keyboard navigation, and high-contrast visuals.  
5. **Compliance with UAE Regulations**: Adherence to Federal Law No. 15 of 2017 on Persons with Disabilities and Dubai’s Accessibility Strategy 2030.  
6. **Inclusive Infrastructure**: Accessible washrooms, signage, and public transportation connectivity.  
7. **User-Centric Data Collection**: Capturing accessibility features (e.g., wheelchair ramp availability, emergency exits) in property listings.  
8. **AI-Driven Accessibility Assessments**: Leveraging technology to evaluate property compliance and suggest improvements.  
9. **Partnerships with Accessibility Providers**: Collaborating with service providers for assistive tech solutions.  
10. **User Feedback Mechanisms**: Collecting insights to refine accessibility offerings and address gaps.  

UAE regulations mandate that public and private sectors ensure accessibility in buildings and digital services. Zaahi’s business model benefits by differentiating itself through inclusive offerings, expanding its customer base, and avoiding legal penalties. Required data points for the platform include property accessibility features, compliance status, user accessibility needs, technology integration details, and metrics on accessibility service utilization.
