3. **Consent** – Explicit, informed, and revocable agreement from data subjects for data processing.  
4. **Data Minimization** – Collection and processing of only necessary data for specific purposes.  
5. **Data Security** – Implementation of technical and organizational measures to safeguard data.  
6. **Data Breach Notification** – Mandatory reporting of breaches within 72 hours to the UAE Data Protection Authority (DPA).  
7. **Data Subject Rights** – Rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data.  
8. **Data Localization** – Storage of personal data within UAE borders unless exceptions apply.  
9. **Accountability** – Organizations must demonstrate compliance through documented policies and audits.  
10. **Data Portability** – Ability of data subjects to transfer their data between service providers.
