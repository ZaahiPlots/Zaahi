# 45 Satellite


45 Satellite is a real estate analytics platform leveraging satellite data and geospatial technology to provide property insights, market trends, and investment analysis. Key concepts include satellite data analytics, urban heat island effect, property valuation models, spatial analysis, environmental impact assessment, land use classification, and risk assessment. UAE regulations require compliance with the Real Estate Regulatory Agency (RERA), Dubai Land Department (DLD), and UAE Personal Data Protection Law (Federal Law No. 38 of 2023). Technology applications include AI-driven data processing, geospatial mapping, IoT integration for real-time property monitoring, and blockchain for data integrity. Business model relevance for Zaahi lies in enhancing property valuation accuracy, supporting investment decisions, and improving market transparency. Required data points include satellite imagery, property metadata, geospatial coordinates, environmental metrics, transaction history, compliance documents, and user preferences.
