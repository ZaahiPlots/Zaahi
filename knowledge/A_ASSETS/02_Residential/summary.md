# 02 Residential


02 Residential refers to residential real estate developments within the 02 district, a mixed-use urban project in Dubai, UAE. Key concepts include freehold and leasehold ownership, zoning regulations, property types (apartments, villas, townhouses), and compliance with UAE legal frameworks. UAE regulations mandate adherence to the Dubai Land Department (DLD) and Real Estate Regulatory Agency (RERA) guidelines, ensuring transparency, consumer protection, and legal validity of transactions. Foreigners may own freehold properties in designated areas like Dubai Marina or Downtown Dubai but are restricted to leasehold in other zones.
