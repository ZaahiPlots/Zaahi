# 36 Auction


36 Auction is an online real estate auction platform integrated into the Zaahi platform, enabling property transactions through competitive bidding in Dubai UAE. Key concepts include auction mechanics, property listings, bidding systems, escrow services, compliance with UAE laws, property valuation, digital marketing, user authentication, post-auction processes, and integration with real estate databases. UAE regulations mandate adherence to the Real Estate Regulatory Agency (RERA) and Dubai Land Department (DLD) guidelines, requiring licenses, transparency, and legal compliance for property sales. Technology applications include blockchain for secure transactions, AI-driven property valuations, mobile bidding apps, cloud-based data storage, and analytics for market trends. The business model leverages digitalization to enhance liquidity, reduce transaction time, and increase transparency, aligning with UAE’s smart city initiatives. Required data points for the platform include property details (location, size, legal status), user authentication credentials, transaction history, market analytics, compliance documents, and integration with DLD databases for real-time data.
