# 80 VR AR


80 VR AR (Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality) in real estate refers to immersive technologies that enable users to visualize properties through 3D environments, interactive overlays, and real-time data integration. Zaahi, a Dubai-based real estate platform, leverages these technologies to enhance property discovery, engagement, and transactions. Key concepts include virtual property tours, augmented overlays for spatial data, 3D modeling, real-time market analytics, and interactive user interfaces. UAE regulations mandate compliance with the Dubai Real Estate Regulatory Authority (DRERA) guidelines, data privacy laws (UAE Personal Data Protection Law), and cybersecurity standards. Technology applications include VR-based property walkthroughs, AR-enhanced site inspections, AI-driven property matching, and blockchain-integrated transactions. Zaahi’s business model benefits from reduced physical visits, increased conversion rates, and competitive differentiation through immersive experiences. Required data points for the platform include property metadata, 3D/VR/AR assets, user interaction logs, real-time market trends, compliance documentation, and analytics for personalization.
