# 62 Legal Engine


62 Legal Engine is a legal technology platform integrated into Zaahi, a Dubai UAE real estate marketplace, designed to automate, standardize, and streamline real estate transaction processes through AI-driven legal compliance and contract management.  

Key concepts: Automated contract generation, real-time compliance checks, risk mitigation tools, integration with UAE real estate laws, smart contract execution, data encryption, real-time property title verification, dispute resolution protocols, user authentication, and audit trail mechanisms.  

UAE regulations: The platform adheres to the UAE Real Estate Law (Federal Law No. 10 of 1981), Dubai Land Department (DLD) guidelines, and the Real Estate Regulatory Agency (RERA) mandates. It ensures compliance with mandatory disclosure requirements, licensing for real estate agents, and data protection under UAE Law No. 38 of 2023 (Personal Data Protection Law).  

Technology applications: Blockchain for immutable contract records, AI-powered legal document analysis, cloud-based secure data storage, API integrations with UAE government databases, and IoT-enabled property data synchronization.  

Business model relevance: The 62 Legal Engine enhances Zaahi’s value proposition by reducing legal transaction costs, minimizing disputes, and improving transparency, aligning with Dubai’s digital transformation goals and real estate market efficiency demands.  

Required data points: Property metadata (type, location, size), legal documents (title deeds, contracts), user credentials (agents, buyers, sellers), transaction history, compliance filings (RERA, DLD), data encryption keys, audit logs, and market-specific legal risk analytics.
