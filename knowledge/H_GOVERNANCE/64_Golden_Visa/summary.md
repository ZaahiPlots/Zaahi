# 64 Golden Visa


64 Golden Visa refers to the UAE's investor residency program requiring a minimum investment of 64 million AED. It grants residency for 10 years, renewable, to foreign investors in sectors like real estate, technology, or industry. Key concepts include: 1. Residency eligibility via substantial investment. 2. Minimum investment threshold of 64 million AED. 3. 10-year residency period with renewable options. 4. Mandatory business license for the investor's entity. 5. Visa-free travel to UAE and 30-day visa-free entry to GCC countries. 6. Access to healthcare and education services. 7. Impact on real estate demand via property purchases by investors. 8. Legal framework under Federal Decree-Law No. 44 of 2020. 9. Integration with real estate platforms for property transactions. 10. Compliance with UAE regulations on foreign ownership and investment. UAE regulations mandate that investors hold a valid business license, operate in approved sectors, and meet financial criteria. Technology applications include blockchain for secure property transactions, AI-driven property matching based on investor profiles, and data analytics to track real estate trends. Zaahi’s business model leverages the Golden Visa by targeting high-net-worth investors, offering tailored real estate services, and integrating legal and compliance tools. Required data points for the platform include investor visa status, investment amount, property preferences, transaction history, legal documentation, and compliance with UAE immigration and real estate laws.
